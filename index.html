Explore Brisbane

Discover the best activities, sights, and experiences in the River City.

Relaxation

South Bank Parklands

Stroll through the lush riverfront parklands, ride the Wheel of Brisbane, or take a dip in Australia's only inner-city, man-made sandy beach.

Adventure

Story Bridge Climb

Face your fears and scale one of Brisbane's most iconic landmarks. Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of the river, city skyline, and Moreton Bay.

Book Your Climb Here
Culture

QAGOMA

Visit the Queensland Art Gallery and Gallery of Modern Art. Located at South Bank, it houses world-class contemporary art exhibitions.

Nature & Cruise

Lone Pine & River Cruise

Enjoy a 2-in-1 experience! Take a leisurely, scenic river cruise departing from South Bank directly to the world's first and largest koala sanctuary to meet native Australian wildlife.

Book Cruise & Entry
Views

Mount Coot-tha Summit

Drive or hike up to the summit lookout for the ultimate panoramic view over Brisbane. Perfect for catching a stunning sunrise or sunset over the city.

Shopping & Dining

Howard Smith Wharves

Nestled directly under the Story Bridge, this vibrant riverfront precinct is packed with fantastic restaurants, local breweries, and lively outdoor seating.
